# About Our Company

## Our Mission

Empowering businesses with intelligent, scalable cloud solutions that drive innovation and growth.

## Company Overview

Founded in 2020, we provide enterprise-grade cloud hosting, data storage, and analytics services to businesses of all sizes—from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

**Key Stats:**
- **Founded**: 2020
- **Headquarters**: San Francisco, California
- **Employees**: 150+ team members globally

- **Uptime**: 99.99% average across all services
- **Data Centers**: 15 locations across 5 continents

## Our Story

### The Beginning (2020)
Founded by former engineers from leading tech companies who saw a need for developer-friendly, enterprise-grade cloud services without enterprise-level complexity.

**Initial Vision:**
- Make powerful cloud tools accessible to all
- No confusing pricing or hidden fees
- Developer experience first
- Enterprise reliability at startup prices

### Growth (2021-2023)
- Launched first data center in San Francisco
- Reached 1,000 customers in first year
- Expanded to Europe and Asia
- Raised Series A funding ($15M)
- Introduced Professional and Enterprise plans

### Today (2024-2025)
- 10,000+ active customers
- 15 global data centers
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- ISO 27001 certified
- Named "Best Cloud Platform" by TechRadar

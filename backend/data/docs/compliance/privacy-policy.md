# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**  
**Effective Date: November 1, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Company ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our services.

By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you don't agree, please don't use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password
- **Profile Information**: Company name, job title, phone number (optional)
- **Payment Information**: Credit card details, billing address (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
- **Communications**: Support tickets, emails, feedback

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies
- **Log Data**: Access times, error logs, API requests

### Information from Third Parties
- Authentication services (Google, GitHub, Microsoft)
- Payment processors (Stripe)
- Analytics providers (opt-in only)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Provide Services**: Deliver and maintain our platform
2. **Account Management**: Create and manage your account
3. **Communications**: Send service updates, respond to inquiries
4. **Billing**: Process payments and send invoices
5. **Security**: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, protect accounts
6. **Improvements**: Analyze usage to enhance our services

### Legal Basis (GDPR)
- **Contract Performance**: Necessary to provide services
- **Legitimate Interest**: Improve services, prevent fraud
- **Consent**: Marketing communications (opt-in)
- **Legal Obligation**: Comply with laws and regulations

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### When We Share Data

**Service Providers:**

## Your Rights

### Access and Portability
- Request copy of your data
- Download your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
- Available in Account Settings > Export Data

### Correction
- Update account information anytime
- Contact support for help with corrections

### Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
- Request account deletion: Account Settings > Delete Account
- Data deleted within 30 days
- Some data retained for legal/security reasons
- Backups purged within 90 days

### Restriction and Objection
- Limit how we use your data
- Object to processing for legitimate interests
- Opt out of marketing communications

### Withdraw Consent
- Revoke cookie consent anytime
- Opt out of analytics
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Account data | While account active + 90 days | Service provision |
| Usage logs | 12 months | Security, debugging |
| Payment records | 7 years | Legal requirement |
| Support tickets | 3 years | Quality assurance |
| Marketing data | Until opt-out | Marketing consent |

**After Retention:**
- Data permanently deleted
- Backups overwritten
- No recovery possible

## Security Measures

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions, multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring, intrusion detection
- **Backups**: Encrypted daily backups, secure storage

### Organizational Safeguards
- Employee training on data protection
- Background checks for staff with data access
- Confidentiality agreements
- Regular security audits

### Your Responsibility
- Strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Don't share account credentials
- Report suspicious activity immediately

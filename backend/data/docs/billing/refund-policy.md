# Refund and Cancellation Policy

## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 30 days of your first purchase if you're not satisfied with our service.

**Eligibility:**
- First-time customers only
- Request within 30 days of initial charge
- Applies to first month or year only
- Service usage doesn't affect eligibility

**Not Eligible:**
- Renewal charges (only first purchase)
- Add-on purchases
- Account terminated for violations
- Refund already received previously

## Requesting a Refund

**Process:**
1. Contact billing support via email: billing@company.com
2. Include:
   - Account email address
   - Transaction ID or invoice number
   - Reason for refund (optional but helpful)
3. Receive confirmation email within 24 hours
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days
5. Refund issued to original payment method

**After Requesting:**
- Account access maintained during processing
- Can export data before cancellation
- Automatic downgrade to free tier (if available)
- Or complete account closure if requested

## Prorated Refunds

**When Available:**
- Billing errors or duplicate charges
- Service downtime exceeding SLA
- Downgrade mid-cycle (credit applied)
- Early cancellation due to company error

**Not Available:**
- Voluntary mid-cycle cancellation
- Change of mind after 30 days
- Partial month usage

## Duplicate Charge Policy

**If charged twice accidentally:**
- Full refund of duplicate charge within 24 hours
- Automatic detection and refund in most cases
- Contact support immediately if not auto-resolved
- No waiting period - processed immediately

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel

**Self-Service Cancellation:**
1. Log into account dashboard
2. Navigate to Billing > Subscription
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Receive confirmation email

**Via Support:**
- Email billing@company.com with "Cancel Subscription"
- Include account email
- Processed within 24 hours

### What Happens When You Cancel

**Immediate Effects:**
- Billing stops at end of current period
- No automatic renewal
- Access maintained until period ends

**End of Billing Period:**
- Account downgraded to free tier (if available)
- Or access restricted if no free tier
- Data preserved for 90 days
- Can reactivate within 90 days without data loss

**After 90 Days:**
- Account data permanently deleted
- Cannot be recovered
- Must create new account to restart

### Cancellation and Refunds

**Monthly Plans:**
- Cancel anytime
- No refund for partial month
- Access until current month ends
- Example: Cancel on March 15, access until March 31

**Annual Plans:**
- Can cancel anytime
- Refund for unused months (after 30-day window)
- Calculated as: (Remaining months / 12) × Annual fee
- Minus 20% processing fee for annual discount

**Example Annual Refund:**
- Annual plan: $758 (Professional)
- Cancel after 3 months (9 months remaining)
- Calculation: ($758 / 12) × 9 = $568.50
- Less 20%: $568.50 × 0.80 = $454.80 refund

## Billing Disputes

**If you believe you were charged incorrectly:**

1. **Contact Us First** (Required before chargeback)
   - Email: billing@company.com
   - Include invoice number and explanation
   - We resolve 95% of issues within 48 hours

2. **Review Your Invoice**
   - Check for add-ons or overage charges
   - Verify billing cycle date
   - Confirm plan level

3. **Common Billing Confusion**
   - Overage charges for exceeding limits
   - Pro-rated charges for upgrades
   - Annual renewal (forgot about commitment)
   - Add-on charges (team members, storage)

**If We Can't Resolve:**
- Full documentation of attempt to resolve
- Right to dispute with your bank
- We provide transaction details
- No penalties for legitimate disputes

**Warning About Chargebacks:**
- Account immediately suspended during dispute
- Service access lost
- Data may be deleted
- Can complicate resolution
- Please contact us first!

## Refund Processing Times

- **Wire Transfer**: 10-14 business days

**What to Expect:**
1. Refund approved (email confirmation)
2. Refund initiated (transaction ID provided)

**Not Showing Up?**
- Check bank processing times
- Verify correct payment method on file
- Contact your bank about incoming refund
- Email us with transaction ID after 10 days

## Special Circumstances

### Service Outages
**If service unavailable beyond SLA:**
- Automatic service credits applied
- Credits equal to: (Downtime hours / Total hours) × Monthly fee
- Example: 24 hours down on $79/mo plan = $2.63 credit
- Applied to next invoice automatically

### Account Termination
**If we terminate account for violation:**
- No refund for current period
- Immediate loss of access
- Data deleted after 30 days
- Appeals considered on case-by-case basis

### Force Majeure
Events beyond our control (natural disasters, war, etc.):
- Services temporarily unavailable
- Pro-rated credits for extended outages
- No refunds for short-term disruptions
- Communication via status page
